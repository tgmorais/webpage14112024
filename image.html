Digital Photography Analysis

Analyze digital photographs to extract valuable information using advanced computer vision techniques. From fruit counting in orchards to identifying grains on a cob, our solutions deliver precision insights.

Key Features

  • Plant Analysis: Detect plant nutrient deficiency, pest and diseases, and weed infestation for optimized management.
  • Fruit Counting: Automatically detect and count fruits on trees.
  • Grain Analysis: Identify and count grains on a cob with high accuracy.
  • Segmentation: Differentiate between fruits, leaves, and background.
  • Object Detection: Detect and label multiple objects in an image.

Applications

  • Agriculture: Optimize harvest estimation and quality control.
  • Food Processing: Automate sorting and counting processes.
  • Research: Facilitate plant science and growth studies.
